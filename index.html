            FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
            • HOW TO PLAY
              1. GATHER RESOURCES
                • Hit trees, rocks and bushes to gather wood, stone and food
                • This resources will allow you to build items and recover life
              2. UPGRADE YOUR CHARACTER
                • By hitting elements, you gather experience
                • At each level, you can unlock a new item or a new weapon
              3. COLLECT GOLD
                • The gold you have represents your score.
                • Collect gold by placing mills, hitting gold in the map or by killing players
            • ACCOUNT
              1. SIGN UP / LOGIN
                • Click on the top right button SIGN UP to create an account
                • Click on the top right button LOGIN to login to an existing account
              2. ACCOUNT FEATURES
                • With an account, you can unlock cosmetics, appears in the global ranking or save your stats
            • RANKING
              1. IN GAME
                • The leaderboard in the game shows who has the most gold currently. The higher your score, the more chances you have to be attacked by other players.
              2. GLOBAL RANKING
                • The global ranking shows the best scores achieved by players with an account. Try to beat them!
            • FEEDBACK OR BUGS REPORT
              1. DISCORD
                • You can share your game experience with the discord community.
                • You can also report bugs or feedback on the game.
                • And finally, you can play with many other players.
